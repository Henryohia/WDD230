Overview

Purpose

The Ikoyi Chamber of Commerce exists to promote community development, prosperity, and economic progress in Lagos. In order to foster cooperation and shared success, we are committed to acting as a unifying factor for neighborhood companies, associations, and entrepreneurs.

Audience

Real estate managers, Investors and Developers, Residents, Tourists, Government and Municipal Authorities, Educational Institutions, Media and Journalists, Researchers and Academics, Marketers, all persons.
